Fullmetal Alchemist

Summary

Al and Ed elric are two brothers on a quest to get their bodies back. Al and Ed lost their bodies by committing the greatest taboo in alchemy human transmutation in order to bring back their dead mother. Alchemy has only one law equivalent exchange meaning something must be given of equal value in order to finish the pact. But what could be equal to a human soul. For that Edward lost a leg and arm while al payed the greatest price of losing his whole body. Now Ed must join the military to find clues on how to recover what has been lost.

The step to alchemy

Alchemy has three steps comprehension, deconstruction and Reconstruction. Comprehension is the act of understanding what the molecular structure is of that object that is desired. Deconstruction is the use of energy used to mold down the physical structure of the material. Reconstruction reforms the material into a new object. The molecules most equal to the molecules started with. One should never try humman transmutation the results are devastating.
